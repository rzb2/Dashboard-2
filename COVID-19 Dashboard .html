Daily New Confirmed Deaths (per capita) - 2020 to 2022

Average Daily New Deaths per Capita (2020 - 2022) - Bar chart:

The average daily new deaths per capita for Sweden and the UK are shown in this bar chart for the years 2020–2022. The y-axis shows the average daily new deaths per capita, and the x-axis shows the countries under comparison.

Sweden: Over the given period, the average daily new deaths per capita are displayed in the blue bar, which represents Sweden. The total number of daily confirmed deaths from COVID-19 divided by Sweden's projected population yields the average daily new deaths per capita.

United Kingdom (UK): The average daily new deaths per capita for the same period are displayed in the red bar, which stands for the UK. Like Sweden, the UK's average daily new deaths per capita are determined by dividing the total number of daily new COVID-19 confirmed deaths by the country's estimated population.

Daily New Deaths per Capita (2020 - 2022) - Line graph:

The daily new deaths per capita for Sweden and the UK from 2020 to 2022 are shown in this line graph. The dates are plotted on the x-axis, while the daily new deaths per capita are plotted on the y-axis.

Sweden: The daily new deaths per capita throughout time are represented by the blue line. For a given date, each point on the line shows the daily new COVID-19-related confirmed deaths per capita.

United Kingdom (UK): Over the same time period, the daily new deaths per capita are displayed by the red line, which represents the UK. Like in Sweden, each point on the graph indicates the number of newly confirmed COVID-19 deaths every day for a certain date.

By comparing the average daily new deaths per capita and the daily new deaths per capita trends between Sweden and the UK, these visualisations provide an analysis of how the COVID-19 pandemic has affected mortality rates in each nation. Both a bar chart and line graph are presented for two forms of visualisation.
